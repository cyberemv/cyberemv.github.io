Introducing the new Open Online Course Platform


Hi, we are looking forward to introducing the new Open Online Course at the doorstep. We help students build relationships with researchers around the world and gain knowledge through their knowledge or research based on a particular topic.

Research is "the creative and systematic work undertaken to increase the accumulation of knowledge". It involves the collection, organization and analysis of information to enhance the understanding of a subject or issue. A research project may be an extension of past activities in this area. Research can be repeated on previous projects or overall components of the project to verify the validity of equipment, procedures or experiments.
